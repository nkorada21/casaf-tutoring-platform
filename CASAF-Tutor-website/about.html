About CASAF Tutoring

Empowering students across Cameroon through quality tutoring, strong mentorship, and community-driven academic support.

Our Mission

CASAF (Community Action Scheme Africa Foundation) is committed to helping students across Cameroon succeed in school and in life. We believe that every learner deserves access to quality education, personalized support and opportunities to thrive.

Through our tutoring program, we connect students with passionate, experienced tutors who guide them academically, help them build confidence and support them in securing top results in their exams.
